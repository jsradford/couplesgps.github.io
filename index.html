What is it?

Using the science of gratitude to strengthen your relationship

Science has shown that gratitude, acknowledging the good things in your life, makes you happier, healthier, and more satisfied with life. The reality is that we're often too busy living our lives to take a step back and appreciate what we have. The work we do in our jobs and in our relationships wears us down when we don't take time for gratitude.

Couples GPS is here to help you and your significant other take the time to recognize the good things you're doing for one another. Whether you're just dating or have been married for 50 years, all couples need to take a moment to be thankful. Couples who are more thankful are happier, more satisfied with their relationship, and more resilient in the face of life's challenges.
